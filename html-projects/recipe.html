Recipe #1: Patbingsu

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Ice 2 pounds
Red Beans 1 pound
Milk 0.5 liters
Rice Cake Pieces 0.5 pounds
Chocolate Sauce 0.2 pounds

Step 1: Crush the ice into fine particles
Step 2: Add milk and red beans onto the crushed ice.
Step 3: Add the rice cake pieces and the chocolate sauce on top of step 2.
Step 4: Mix all of the ingredients together with a spoon.
Step 5: Enjoy!

Recipe #2: Fruit Bings
